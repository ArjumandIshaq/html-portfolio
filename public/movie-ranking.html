The Best Movies According to Arjumand

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Heropanti

"Heropanti" is Bollywood film marking the debut of young stars Tiger Shroff and Kriti Sanon.

Half Girlfriend

"Half Girlfriend" is a Bollywood romantic drama released in 2017, based on Chetan Bhagat's novel of the same name.

Hacker

"Hacker" is a gripping techno-thriller film that delves into the clandestine world of hacking and cybercrime.
